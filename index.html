It was a dark and stormy night...

A lantern rested on an old oak table illuminating a dusty cabin with an exhausted man sleeping on a cot.

The man was uneasily awoken by the sound of a crow chirping in the distance.

Accustomed to sleeping light for predators and vanguards, he scanned the area before realizing that the sound was something else.

The old man however was already awake, and sleeping does not come easy to him. The man rises from his cot.
